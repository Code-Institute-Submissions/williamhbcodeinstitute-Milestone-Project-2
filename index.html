Travel Vietnam

Looking for an exotic destination? How about Vietnam for your next holiday? Vietnam is a Southeast Asian country on the South China Sea known for its beaches, rivers, Buddhist pagodas and bustling cities. Hanoi, the capital, pays homage to the nation’s iconic Communist-era leader, Ho Chi Minh, via a huge marble mausoleum. Ho Chi Minh City (formerly Saigon) has French colonial landmarks, plus Vietnamese War history museums and the Củ Chi tunnels, used by Viet Cong soldiers.

In this unique travel package, you will journey through Vietnam starting at the historical city Ho-Chi-Minh in the south. Your journey will continue through central Vietnamese cities including Hue City and Da Nang City (All cities can be located on the map below).
